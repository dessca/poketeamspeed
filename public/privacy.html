Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

SCARF does not require accounts and does not ask visitors to submit personal information.

Saved teams, settings, language, theme, and view preferences may be stored in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device unless you clear your browser data.

SCARF uses Vercel Analytics to understand aggregate site usage and improve the tool. Vercel Web Analytics is designed to work without cookies and uses aggregated data.

Advertising

If ads are shown, third-party vendors including Google may use cookies to serve ads and measure performance. You can manage personalized ads through Google Ads Settings.
